Welcome

Luxosun is a family business located on the Côte d'Azur and Monaco. We offer luxury on-demand services such as concierge service, maintenance of your main and secondary residences while keeping your pets during your prolonged absences.

Preparation of your arrival such as organising household cleaning before arrival and end of stay, shopping, organising weekends on request and many more.
A sense of security throughout the year with our regular visits service, as well as the possibility of a tailored and personalised annual contract.
For residents who go on holiday or even on an extended weekend, we take care of your pets at your home. From simple visits to regular walks, we meet your needs.
Yearly management of your secondary residence according to your needs, such as multiple repairs, garden maintenance, pool maintenance and others.
